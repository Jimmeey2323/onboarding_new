Embodying Core Values

  • Establish eye contact immediately to convey attention and respect.
  • Always wear a warm smile to foster a welcoming atmosphere.
  • Adopt open and approachable body language.
  • Modulate your tone and volume to match the client’s comfort.
  • Strive to be relatable and approachable in all interactions.
  • Keep the environment light and enjoyable.

Client Greetings

  • Treat every client as a top priority, ensuring they feel valued and welcomed.
  • Greet each client naturally as you would a new acquaintance, ensuring the approach is genuine and not overpowering.
  • Proactively introduce yourself; don't wait for clients to make the first move.

Importance of Learning Client Names

  • Remembering a client’s name personalizes the experience, showing care and commitment.
  • Familiarity helps in building enduring relationships with regular clients, enhancing the community feel of Physique 57.
  • These personal connections distinguish our service, contributing significantly to the client's overall experience.

Physique Tip for Recalling Names

  • “Hi there! How are you today? Remind me of your name?”
  • “Hello! I don’t believe we’ve met yet, could I have your name?”
  • “Thank you! You’re in studio X with X today—enjoy your class!”
