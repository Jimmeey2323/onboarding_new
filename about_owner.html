About the Owner

Mallika Parekh, an accomplished entrepreneur with a profound dedication to health and wellness, is the driving force behind the introduction of Physique 57 to the Indian fitness landscape. With an educational background that merges the disciplines of public health and business, Parekh has always been passionate about empowering individuals through wellness and preventive health measures. Her journey to revolutionize the fitness industry in India began after witnessing the transformative effects of Physique 57, a New York-based barre fitness company known for its innovative and effective workout programs.

Recognizing the potential for such a holistic and empowering fitness regimen in India, where the wellness market was ripe for a high-intensity, yet low-impact workout option, Parekh embarked on her mission to bring Physique 57 to her home country. In [year], after meticulous planning and collaboration with Physique 57's founders, Parekh successfully opened the first studio in Mumbai, marking a significant milestone in India's fitness industry.

Parekh's vision extended beyond just introducing a new workout to the market; she aimed to create a community centered around wellness, where individuals could come together to work on their physical fitness while also embracing a healthier lifestyle. Her efforts have not only popularized barre fitness in India but also cultivated a space where clients feel supported and motivated in their fitness journeys.

Through her leadership, Physique 57 India has flourished, offering a diverse range of classes that cater to different fitness levels and preferences, making the studio a beloved destination for those seeking a unique, effective, and enjoyable workout experience. Mallika Parekh's story is one of passion, perseverance, and the pursuit of wellness, illustrating how visionary leadership can introduce innovative health solutions to new markets.

Since 2005, Parekh has worked on the cutting edge of science and healthcare. Beginning her career in research at New York Presbyterian Hospital and Massachusetts General Hospital, Parekh earned a double Masters in Medical Science and Public Health. Parekh then served as an advisor to healthcare systems and hospitals in the US, as well as worked on strategy in healthcare finance.
